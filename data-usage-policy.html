Data Usage Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Purpose and Scope

This Data Usage Policy outlines the terms and conditions for accessing and using research data, databases, and tools provided by the Applied Science Research Institute. This policy applies to all users, including researchers, academics, students, and institutions accessing our research resources.

2. Data Access

Access to our research data is provided under the following conditions:

  • Academic and Research Use: Data is primarily intended for academic research, educational purposes, and non-commercial scientific inquiry.
  • Registration Required: Access to certain datasets may require registration and approval of a data access request.
  • Institutional Affiliation: Some datasets may be restricted to researchers affiliated with academic or research institutions.
  • Purpose Statement: Users must provide a clear statement of research purpose when requesting data access.

3. Permitted Uses

Our research data may be used for:

  • Academic research and scholarly publication
  • Educational purposes in academic settings
  • Non-commercial scientific analysis and replication studies
  • Policy research and evidence-based decision making
  • Development of open-source research tools and methodologies

4. Prohibited Uses

The following uses of our data are strictly prohibited:

  • Commercial use without explicit written permission
  • Re-identification of anonymized data
  • Use for surveillance, tracking, or monitoring of individuals
  • Use that violates applicable laws or regulations
  • Redistribution of data without proper attribution
  • Use that could harm individuals or groups
  • Use for competitive intelligence or commercial advantage

5. Data Attribution and Citation

When using our research data, you must provide proper attribution. All publications, presentations, or other works using our data must include:

  • Citation of the original data source: "Applied Science Research Institute Research Database"
  • Reference to relevant publications or documentation
  • Acknowledgment of the Applied Science Research Institute in acknowledgments or credits
  • Link to our website (appresearch.org) when publishing online

Example Citation:

Data from Applied Science Research Institute Research Database. Applied Science Research Institute. https://appresearch.org. Accessed [Date].

6. Data Integrity and Modifications

Users must maintain the integrity of the data and may not:

  • Modify, alter, or manipulate data in ways that misrepresent the original findings
  • Remove attribution, metadata, or documentation
  • Combine our data with other datasets in ways that could compromise privacy or data quality
  • Create derivative datasets without proper documentation of modifications

7. Privacy and Confidentiality

All users must respect privacy and confidentiality requirements:

  • Do not attempt to re-identify anonymized data
  • Do not share data with unauthorized parties
  • Implement appropriate security measures when storing or processing data
  • Report any potential privacy breaches or data security incidents immediately
  • Comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations

8. Research Tools and Software

Our research tools and software are provided under specific license terms:

  • Open-source tools are typically provided under MIT, Apache, or similar permissive licenses
  • Proprietary tools may require separate licensing agreements
  • Users must comply with all license terms and restrictions
  • Modifications to tools should be contributed back to the community when possible

9. Data Updates and Versioning

Our research databases are periodically updated with new data and corrections:

  • Users should check for updated versions before beginning new research
  • Version numbers and change logs are provided with each dataset
  • Users should cite the specific version of data used in their research
  • Older versions may be archived but may not reflect current findings

10. Limitations and Disclaimers

Users acknowledge that:

  • Data is provided "as is" without warranties of any kind
  • We do not guarantee accuracy, completeness, or fitness for any particular purpose
  • Users are responsible for validating data for their specific use cases
  • We are not liable for any consequences arising from data use
  • Data may contain errors or limitations that should be acknowledged in publications

11. Violations and Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:

  • Immediate revocation of data access privileges
  • Legal action if violations involve misuse of data or breach of agreements
  • Notification to institutional affiliations of serious violations
  • Public disclosure of policy violations when appropriate

12. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in data availability, legal requirements, or best practices. Users will be notified of significant changes, and continued use of our data constitutes acceptance of updated terms.

13. Contact and Support

For questions about data access, usage, or this policy, please contact:

Data Access Requests: research@appresearch.org
Policy Questions: info@appresearch.org
Technical Support: research@appresearch.org

14. Agreement

By accessing or using our research data, tools, or databases, you agree to comply with this Data Usage Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, you must not access or use our research resources.
